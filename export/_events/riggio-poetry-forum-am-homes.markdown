Tom will moderate a forum discussion with A.M. Homes, author of the novels, *This Book Will Save Your Life*, *Music For Torching*, *The End of Alice*, *In a Country of Mothers*, and *Jack*, as well as the short-story collections, *Things You Should Know*, *The Safety of Objects*, and the best-selling memoir *The Mistress's Daughter*. Homes' work has been translated into twenty-two languages and appears frequently in Artforum, Harper's, Granta, McSweeney's, The New Yorker, The New York Times, and Zoetrope. She is a contributing editor to Vanity Fair, Bomb and Blind Spot.
